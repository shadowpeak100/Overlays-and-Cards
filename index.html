The mountains to me

The mountains and nature had always held a special place to me. There is a certain joy that I get looking down at my phone and seeing that I have no reception whatsoever. It's the best way that I can relax and exploring new places always brings me joy. This page shows some of the adventures I have had and highlights the value of them to me.

What should I do to get out in nature?

Getting out in nature is surprisingly easy, the most important thing you can do is to just go. The activities are endless and you can bring anyone with you. Making memories out camping, hiking or climbing can be some of the best since you aren't burdened by the weight of the world when you are out away from society.

Some suggested activities

Take a hike
It doesn't have to be long, just explore a new place
Mountain bike
Pick a trail and head out, biking can be fast paced and exciting.
Ski
It's even better than snowboarding!

Why is it worth my time?

  • Recharging: most everyone I have talked to has significant stresses from home and work, this is a great way to gain perspective.
  • Change it up: breaking away from the routine of life to do something fun can bring out the best in people.
  • Health benefits: not only can getting out be fun it can bring some benefits:
    • Lower heart rate: spending time in thinner mountain air can reduce heart rate and extend life.
    • Improved body condition: hiking and the likes can help you tone up and stay in shape, in an exciting way.
